Get started with Tradeshift

To help you get started on Tradeshift and to allow you to make the most of our features, here are some useful tips.

Upload Your Company Logo

Upload your logo on the 'Profile' page. It will be placed in the header of each of your invoices and also be shown when collaborating with other companies.

Pro Tip: Get more new business by highlighting what you offer on your 'Profile' page. This way companies can search for your products and connect with you on Tradeshift.

How to Create Your Invoices More Efficiently

To send an invoice, click the green 'Create Invoice' button on Tradeshift. Add a recipient and complete the details of your invoice. Search for invoices sent or received at any time from the 'Documents' tab.

Pro Tip: To save more time, click on an invoice from your Documents, then click on 'use as draft' to use it as a template.
